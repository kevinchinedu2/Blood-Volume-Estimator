About Blood Volume Estimator

This project was born out of a desire to merge clinical accuracy with modern web technology. By using established medical formulas, this tool provides a seamless way for users to understand their physiological metrics.

The backend is powered by a robust MySQL architecture, ensuring that every height and weight entry is processed securely and stored for your future reference.

As I continue to develop this platform, the goal remains the same: to provide a fast, reliable, and accessible health utility that works perfectly on both desktop and mobile devices.

Thank you for using the tool. Stay tuned for future updates as we transition toward a dedicated mobile application experience.
